Ogranizational Commitment Post-Layoffs

This was my final research project for my Industrial Organizational Psychology Masters Degree. In the midst of the COVID-19 pandemic (April 2020-April 2021), I chose to research organizational commitment and turnover intentions among employees who survived company layoffs. For this project, I researched related studies, designed and administered a survey, built and tested a hypothesis, and then used IBM SPSS Statistics to run models and visualize the data.
